About Our Project

We participated in a 12-hour hackathon at GMRIT College and successfully developed an elective course selection website. This platform allows students to select courses from other departments, enabling them to explore different fields and enhance their knowledge.

Our project is aimed at making course selection easier and more dynamic, ensuring that students have access to a wider range of learning opportunities.
